Setup

Before you begin, ensure your robot is powered on and the control program is running.

Configuration

Configure your robot by following these steps:

  1. Access the settings page.
  2. Enter the IP address and port number of your robot.

Control Your Robot

Once configured, control your robot with these controls:

  • Use WASD keys for body movement.
  • Use QERF keys for head movement.
